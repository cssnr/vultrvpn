About

Create the VPN... Use the VPN... Delete the VPN...

This product provides you with an ephemeral VPN for less than $0.01 per hour (that is correct, 1 penny per hour).

Prerequisites

Before you begin you will need the following:

  1. Free Vultr Account: https://www.vultr.com
  2. OpenVPN Client: https://openvpn.net/download-open-vpn

    3. Downloads for Windows, Mac, Linux, and Mobile under the "OpenVPN Connect" section.

How to Use

  1. Whitelist our IP address and copy your API token at Vultr.

    2. https://my.vultr.com/settings/#settingsapi

    {% get_config 'PUBLIC_IP' %}/32

  2. Paste in your token, choose a location, and click the Create VPN button.

    3. Go to Home Page to Create VPN

  3. You will be redirected to page with the status of your VPN and download link to the VPN file.

    4. Your VPN will take 1-2 minutes to setup, then you can download the file, connect and use the VPN.
    Once the VPN file is downloadable, the VPN should be ready to use.

  4. When you are done, click the Delete VPN button. That is all.
